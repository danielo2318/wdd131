SitePlan - Beatboard

Site's Name

Beatboard

Beatboard is a personal site that shares recommendations from bands and favorite music. The name combines the word “beat” with “board” (panel or space), reflecting a place where sounds are exposed recommended.

Site's Purpose

Beatboard is meant to recommend bands that I like and share basic information about them, like favorite songs, important albums and links to listen to your music. It will also include a dynamic section with weekly recommendations using JavaScript.

Scenarios

  • Which band do you recommend this week?
  • Where can I listen to a song or watch a video of this band?
  • Which bands sound similar to my current tastes?

Color scheme

  • Orange (#ff6600): For titles, buttons and accents.
  • Negro/gris oscuro (#333333): For main text and logo.
  • White and Yellow Cream (#ffffff/#fef7e8): General background to maintain clarity.

Tipografía

  • Montserrat: For headers (modern and eye-catching).
  • Open Sans: For paragraphs and body of the site (clean and readable).

Wireframe diagram

Mobile View:

  • Header with logo and drop-down navigation
  • Featured image or recommended band
  • List of bands with links and images
  • Footer with networks

Desktop View::

  • Fixed horizontal navigation bar
  • Sections divided into grids or columns, like a gallery
  • Dynamic area for weekly recommendation
  • Footer with additional links and references page
